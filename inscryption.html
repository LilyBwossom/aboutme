Inscryption

I've been modding Inscryption since 2021 and I have worked on many mods in that time. The biggest projects that I've worked on are the JSONLoader mod, the AllTheSigils mod and the API.

What I have contributed to the aforementioned mods:
  • JSONCardLoader, I am currently the only maintainer of this mod, I have added support for the creation of sigils, starter decks and encounters.
  • AllTheSigils, I am currently the only maintainer of this mod, I have created a little over half of the sigils in this mod
  • API, I added support for adding more than 3 sigils to a card

In addition to contributing to these mods, I have also made some mods completely by myself. These mods can be found under the team name Lily on the thunderstore website.
