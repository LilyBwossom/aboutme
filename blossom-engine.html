Blossom Engine

Blossom Engine is a friday night funkin engine build on top of version 0.7.3 of psych engine. The goal of Blossom Engine is to make your favorite mods more accessible, significantly reduce file sizes, and maximize fun.

Blossom engine includes the following features:
  • a large amount of downloadable mods/modpacks that have been tweaked to work flawless with the engine
  • categories for each mod in the freeplay menu
  • difficulty ratings for each song and mod category (work in progress)
  • an option to favorite songs so they appear in the "favorites" category
  • an option to select what mechanics to use in a song
  • a gameplay modifier to play as the opponent and to play both sides at once
  • a gameplay modifier to randomize note positions
  • a gameplay modifier to make opponent notes deal damage
  • a gameplay modifier to make the camera move when you hit a note
  • faster menu animations
  • higher limit for the framerate setting
  • a setting to add an underlay beneath the notes for better visibility
  • a setting to start a countdown whenever you resume after pausing a song
  • some small quality of life sprite improvements
  • lua support for all features

You can find a download for blossom engine on gamebanana or gamejolt, and the download page for mods/modpacks created for this engine on this page.
